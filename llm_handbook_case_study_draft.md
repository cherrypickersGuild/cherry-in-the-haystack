# LLM Engineering Case Study Content Map

## 1. 프롬프트, 코드, 워크플로우, MCP, Agent
> Prompts, Code, Workflows, Model Context Protocol (MCP), Agents

- **Prompt Engineering**: Techniques, patterns, and optimization strategies.
- **Code & Implementation**: Scaffolding, architectural patterns for LLM apps.
- **Workflows**: Validated chains, RAG pipelines, and orchestration.
- **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**: Standards, implementations, and connectivity.
- **Agents**: Autonomous agents, swarm architectures, and tooling.

---

## 2. 유즈케이스 (산업별, 도메인별 활용사례, 비즈니스 케이스)
> Use Cases (Industry-specific, Domain-specific, Business Cases: ROI, Strategy, Success Stories)

- **Industry Specific**: Healthcare, Finance, Legal, Manufacturing, etc.
- **Domain Specific**: Customer Support, Content Generation, Data Analysis.
- **Business Cases**:
  - **ROI Analysis**: Cost-benefit breakdowns of AI adoption.
  - **Adoption Strategy**: Roadmaps for integrating LLMs into existing biz.
  - **Success Stories**: Verified implementation reports and outcomes.

---

## 3. 생태계 거버넌스, 지식확산, 감사, 리스크 평가, 정책
> Ecosystem Governance, Knowledge Dissemination, Audit, Risk Assessment, Policy

- **Governance**: Organizational structures for AI oversight.
- **Knowledge Dissemination**: Internal training, documentation culture.
- **Audit & Compliance**: AI system auditing frameworks.
- **Risk Assessment**: Safety, hallucination checks, bias evaluation.
- **Policy & Regulations**: Global and local AI regulations (EU AI Act, etc.).

---

## 4. AI 관련 좋은 글 (일반/개발), 빅테크 동향, 통찰력
> General Good Reads, Big Tech Trends, AI Development, Insights

- **General Reads**: High-quality articles and thought pieces.
- **Big Tech Trends**: Updates from OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Meta, etc.
- **AI Development**: Core research breakthroughs, new model releases.
- **Insights**: Deep dives into the future of AI and strategic implications.
